Who is Elise Aitchison

If you’ve somehow stumbled upon this website: Hey, I’m Elise and I love to make videogames. At the time of writing, I’m currently getting my degree at Falmouth University (and enjoying every second of it). I make an effort with each game I make to either learn a new skill personally or try and create an innovative game within the genera (no simple platformers or shooters here!). When it comes to creating games, I am a champion of “I’ll figure it out”. I’m not afraid to plunge myself into the deep end and try and learn whatever I need to create my vision a reality. This line of thinking has allowed me to pick up a whole verity of skills such as working in VR and using Arduinos. It’s also allowed me to pick up some other skills such as simple animation, pixel art, shaders and more that I’ve used in my solo projects and to assist people in group projects. Stick around and see what wacky idea I'll make next!

If you want to get in contact with me, feel free to email ellsaitchison@gmail.com
